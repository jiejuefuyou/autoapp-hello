# Privacy Policy

**AutoChoice** — last updated 2026-04-29

## What we collect

**Nothing.** AutoChoice does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The app does not require an account, does not contact any server, does not include analytics SDKs, and does not include third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

## What's stored on your device

Your decision lists, choices, theme preference, and (for Premium users) decision history are stored locally in the app's sandboxed Documents directory. This data never leaves your device. Deleting the app removes all stored data.

## Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple via StoreKit. AutoChoice receives only the entitlement status (purchased / not purchased). Apple's own privacy policy governs purchase information: <https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/>.

## Children

AutoChoice is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not collect any data, so no data about children is ever processed.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will appear at this URL with the revised date.
